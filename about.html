ABOUT ME

I’m Matt Tartaglione, a Software Engineer who specializes in full-stack web development. I know a multitude of languages including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.Js, and React just to name a few. I also make a mean old fashioned, if you're into that sort of thing.

A computer nerd at heart, I’ve dabbled in coding and programming throughout my life. In late 2021, I decided to dive head-first into my passion of web development and haven’t looked back. With a strong background in law enforcement, my goal is to have my past experience and proficiency in coding come together like Voltron. That would be pretty dope, right?

If you couldn’t tell by my last name, I come from a big, loud Italian family and wouldn’t have it any other way. Growing up most people called me Tart, and the nickname stuck with me. Outside of being a software engineer I’m an avid hiker, a diehard gamer, a pizza-making aficionado, and a huge movie buff. If I’m doing anything else, it probably involves food...

I’m currently based in Connecticut with my beautiful wife, Mallory, and our golden retriever, Kylo (yes, of course that’s a Star Wars reference).
