About Us

Nestled on the banks of the Farmington River, Legends offers comfortable rooms in a lodge setting with river views, breakfast Thursday-Sunday, healthy afternoon snacks and a special homemade juice of the day. All rooms offer AC, private bath, TV and internet. Fly-fishing, hunting kayaking, hiking, mountain/road biking and snowshoeing tours available through Yellow Lab Adventures.

Beautiful Scenery

Legends is a beautiful lodge located 100 feet from the Farmington River. We’re located on the Legendary Greenwoods Pool. Flyfish in our backyard or relax and watch the river come alive from one of our comfortable private decks overlooking the beautiful Farmington river.

And when you’re ready to relax indoors, relax with friends and family in one of our luxurious community areas or retreat to your private room for afternoon tea,coffee or a refreshing cocktail and a good book.

Legends Bed and Breakfast offers spacious rooms and suites with private bath, TV and internet. Direct river access and ample off street parking. Flyfish, hike, kayak, ski, go antiquing or enjoy a movie at one of Connecticuts last Drive Ins; all in walking distance from your room.
